Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our booking system. The system was developed as part of a school project and follows basic data protection principles.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you interact with our system:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and phone number provided when making a reservation.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the system (e.g., time and date of reservations).

3. How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • To process and manage reservations.
  • To improve the functionality of the system.
  • To contact users regarding their reservations.

4. Data Sharing

Your personal data will not be shared with third parties unless required by law or for the purposes of completing a reservation process.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, please note that no system is entirely secure.
